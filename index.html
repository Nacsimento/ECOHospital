Service Offered

Path Labs

Path labs are equipped with advanced medical equipment and staffed by skilled technicians and pathologists who perform a wide range of tests

CT Scan

A CT scan can show detailed images of any part of the body, including the bones, muscles, organs and blood vessels.

X Ray

X-rays are valuable for imaging bones, identifying fractures, joint dislocations, and skeletal issues. They're also employed for lung examinations

MRI Scan

MRI scans, using strong magnets and radio waves, create high-resolution images of internal structures. Valuable for diagnosing soft tissue injuries and tumors.

Pharmacy

Pharmacies dispense prescribed medications, offer health consultations, and provide over-the-counter remedies. Crucial in patient care and contribute significantly to public well-being.

Parking
